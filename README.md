# Car-Price-Analysis-and-Time-Series-Forecasting-for-Australian-car-company
Project Overview

This project integrates regression analysis and time series forecasting to examine historical car pricing patterns and predict future stock trading volumes. The objective was to combine cross-sectional price analysis with temporal forecasting techniques to support strategic pricing and market planning decisions.

By leveraging statistical modeling and trend analysis, the project delivers both explanatory insights and forward-looking projections.

Business Objective

Identify key factors influencing vehicle pricing

Analyze historical pricing and market trends

Forecast future stock trading volumes using time series techniques

Support pricing strategy and long-term market planning

Provide data-driven recommendations for executive decision-making

Methodology
1. Regression Analysis

Correlation analysis to identify significant relationships

Multiple regression modeling to quantify pricing drivers

Interpretation of coefficients for business insight

Model validation and diagnostic testing

2. Time Series Forecasting

Trend identification using exponential trend models

Pattern analysis of historical trading volumes

Forecast generation based on historical growth trends

Evaluation of forecasting performance

Key Insights

Quantified the impact of selected predictors on vehicle pricing

Identified strong correlations between market variables

Revealed underlying growth trends in stock trading volumes

Generated forward-looking forecasts to guide strategic planning

Business Impact

The combined analytical approach enables:

More accurate pricing strategies

Better inventory and market positioning decisions

Improved forecasting for financial and operational planning

Enhanced understanding of market behavior over time

Tools and Technologies

Python

Pandas and NumPy

Statsmodels

Scikit-learn
